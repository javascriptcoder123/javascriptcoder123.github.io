❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my account?

Go to the login screen and tap “Forgot Password” to reset your credentials.

How do I delete my account?

Contact support and request account deletion. We will remove your data within a reasonable timeframe.

The app is crashing. What should I do?

Make sure you are using the latest version of Spark. If the issue continues, contact support with your device details.
